Mercury

Position: Closest planet to the Sun.

Characteristics: Mercury has a very thin atmosphere and experiences extreme temperature variations, ranging from about 430°C during the day to -180°C at night. Its surface is heavily cratered.

Detail: Since Mercury has almost no atmosphere, it cannot protect itself from impacts by meteoroids, comets, and other celestial bodies. As a result, Mercury's surface has been shaped by numerous collisions over billions of years, leaving behind many impact craters. One of the most notable features is the Caloris Basin, the largest impact crater on Mercury, with a diameter of about 1,550 kilometers.
