Jupiter

Position: Fifth planet from the Sun.

Characteristics: Jupiter is the largest planet in the solar system, composed mainly of gas and liquid. It has a Great Red Spot, a massive storm, and about 79 moons.

Rotation: It has a rapid rotation period of about 10 hours and a revolution period of 12 Earth years.
