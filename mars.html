Mars

Position: Fourth planet from the Sun.

Characteristics: Mars is known for its reddish appearance due to iron oxide (rust). It has a thin atmosphere and ice caps at its poles. Evidence suggests that liquid water once flowed on its surface.

Rotation: Its rotation period is about 24.6 hours, and its revolution period is 687 Earth days.
