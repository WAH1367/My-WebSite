Venus

Position: Second planet from the Sun.

Characteristics: Venus has a thick atmosphere rich in carbon dioxide, resulting in a strong greenhouse effect. The average surface temperature is around 460°C, and its surface is very rugged.

Rotation: It rotates once every 243 Earth days, which is longer than its revolution period, and it rotates in a retrograde direction.
